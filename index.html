Deadpool

Deadpool is a comic book character from the Marvel Comics. He was created by artist Rob Liefeld and writer Fabian Nicieza. His first appearance was in New Mutants #98. He was originally a villain when he was first created, but soon became more of a mercenary and anti-hero in later comics.

One of Deadpool's many comic book appearances was when "Deadpool Kills The Marvel Universe". This was one part of an entire series of comics on Deadpool where he tried to rebel against the creators of comic book heroes and villians. As usual Deadpool attempts to break the 'fourth wall' through his actions and motives.
